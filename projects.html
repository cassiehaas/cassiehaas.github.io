Northern Illinois Stormwater Storage Prediction

In January of 2025, I began working with the Chicago Metropolitan Agency for Planning to use neural networks to help predict where stormwater would gather with the intent to improve future storage systems and secure budgets in the future. My particular work involved researching and implementing a Digital Elevation Model of the area, improving model accuracy significantly.
